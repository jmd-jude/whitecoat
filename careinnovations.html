Real-World Example: IBD Nutrition Navigation

The Challenge

Inflammatory Bowel Disease patients need highly personalized nutrition guidance, but dietitians with IBD expertise are scarce and expensive. Patients often wait months for appointments and struggle with generic advice that doesn't account for their specific symptoms, triggers, and lifestyle.

Our Solution

Working with IBD nutrition specialists, we've created an AI-powered consultation experience that provides expert-level, personalized guidance in real-time:

  • Conducts thorough intake interviews accounting for individual symptoms and triggers
  • Provides personalized meal planning and nutrition strategies
  • Generates detailed action plans and monitoring tools
  • Adapts recommendations as patients track their progress

The Impact

Rather than waiting months for specialist appointments or relying on generic online tools, patients receive immediate access to expert-level guidance that scales in ways that traditional one-on-one consultations simply cannot. This allows specialists to focus their direct time on the most complex cases requiring human intervention.

Potential Application: Medicaid Navigation

Medicaid systems share many characteristics that make them potentially well-suited for this approach:

  • Complex, context-specific rules: Eligibility requirements vary by state, family situation, income changes, and life circumstances
  • High-stakes decisions: People need accurate guidance to access essential healthcare
  • Existing expertise gap: There's a cottage industry of consultants and specialists, but not nearly enough to meet demand
  • Equity imperative: Those who most need help often have least access to expert guidance

Our preliminary testing with Massachusetts Medicaid data suggests the platform could effectively guide people through eligibility questions, application processes, and benefit navigation—potentially making expert-level assistance available to anyone who needs it.
